Kedah, Malaysia

Hometown

Kedah Darul Aman

Kedah, known as "Darul Aman" (Abode of Peace), is one of the oldest states in Malaysia, rich in history and culture. Famous for its picturesque landscapes, including vast rice fields, hills, and serene beaches, Kedah is a treasure trove of natural beauty. The state is also home to various historical sites, such as the Kedah Royal Museum and the iconic Langkawi Island, making it a popular destination for both local and international tourists. With its unique blend of tradition and modernity, Kedah remains a significant cultural hub in Malaysia.
